Skincare Finder

Affordable skincare solution

MAR. 2020

The skincare market is inundated with various choices, but the excess amount of choices and available information online does not make current market more advanced than before. Emphasis on the healthy aspects of skincare has been increasing over the years, with companies and consumers responding by attentions towards ingredients and ‘clean‘ products. However, many informations regarding how to select individual skincare are either too opaque and esoteric to users or are reviews too subjective for them to use. Without the appropriate skincare, the trial-and-error period can waste a lot of money and resources. It can also result in skin damage or allergy, such as misuse of exfoliators. Through research that analyzing existing solutions and common user behaviors, this project propose an application that help users build their suitable skincare routine under their needs and preference.
